\section{Two small categories}

Objects: 1, 2

Morphisms: 

1 -> 1: id1
2 -> 2: id2
1 -> 2: f
2 -> 1: (empty set)

Composition:
  id1 . id1 = id1
  id2 . id2 = id2
  f . id1 = f
  id2 . f = f

Right unit: f . id1 = f
Left unit: id2 . f = f

Associativity: as we only compose with identity, the associativity is trivial,

   id2 . f . id1 (the only 3 morphisms) = (id2 . f) . id1 = id2 . (f . id1) = f
